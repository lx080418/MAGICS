Hello Students

Welcome to M.A.G.I.C.S.

This is a non-governmental organization built by students for students that provides a fun and interactive method to approach the tedious and challenging to most subject of mathematics. (Promoting the magic of mathematics in all young minds)

Best Curriculum

Every lecture uses a curriculum that is interactive and well planned out ahead of time to ensure best use of time during lectures.

Best Instructors

Every instructor is a top student at high school, outperforming nearly all other high school students at both academics and competitions such as AIME.

Best Students

Every student embodies the traits of consistency and resilience through their unwillingness to give up on challenging and tedious subjects.

Best Mode of Instruction

Every lecture is in-person instruction, which means it takes place in a physical classroom to ensure maximum concentration and absorption of knowledge.

Best Networking

Every instructor is easily accessible both during lectures through verbal questions and outside of lectures through emails.

Is previous knowledge of mathematics required?

No! We welcome anyone, this is and introductory course with the main pupose of eliciting interest. You current level of mathematics would never be a problem in having fun!

What should I bring with me?

Ideally a computer, but don't let that be a barrier, becuase we provide computers to low-income students. If we don't hae enough computers, we will rotate when playing the educational video game Math Knight!

What is the class size?

Most of the time, around 20 students, 2-3 teachers and 4 volunteers that helps taking care of our students. Pictures of our classroom can be found in Classroom.
